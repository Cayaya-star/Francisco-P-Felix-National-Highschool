About Us

Welcome to Francisco P. Felix Memorial National High School! Our institution is dedicated to providing high-quality education, fostering innovation, and nurturing the potential of every student...... With a history of academic excellence, we aim to empower our learners to achieve success and make a positive impact in their communities.

Our Mission: To create a supportive learning environment where students can excel academically, develop critical thinking skills, and grow into responsible citizens.

Our Vision: To be a leading institution that inspires excellence, creativity, and leadership.

Our Academic Activities & Events

SCHOOL BASED CAMPOREE 2025

February 28 - March 1, 2025

The F.P.Felix - Karangalan Annex successfully conducted the School Based Camporee.


Our School-based Camporee was an unforgettable adventure where teamwork, leadership, and fun came together in the great outdoors. From conquering challenges to gathering around campfires, every moment was filled with learning, growth, and bonding. Scouts tested their skills, formed lasting friendships, and created memories that will last a lifetime. The spirit of camaraderie was stronger than ever, proving that when we work together, we can achieve anything. This Camporee was a true celebration of Scout Values--adventure, resilience, and community.

YES O ACTIVITY 2025

March 11, 2025

Tulong-tulong na Pagsasaayos ng Gulayan sa Paaralan!


Isang masayang araw para sa ating mga mag-aaral at guro sa FPFMNHS - Karangalan Annex! Sa pagtutulungan ng mga Mag-aaral, YES-O, Boy Scouts, Girl Scouts, at ang kanilang mga Guro at Advisers, matagumpay nating naayos at napaganda ang ating Gulayan sa Paaralan. Sa bawat hakbang at pag-aalaga, nagsisilbing inspirasyon ang mga kabataang ito sa pagpapahalaga sa kalikasan at sa pagtutulungan para sa mas magandang bukas. Ang mga gulay at halamang itinanim ay hindi lamang magbibigay sustansya, kundi magsisilbing simbolo ng ating kolektibong pagsisikap at dedikasyon.

EXPLORING THE WONDERS OF SCIENCE

March 3, 2025

Our learners are having an exciting time exploring the wonders of science in our Science Laboratory!


With hands-on experiments and engaging activities, our students are not just learning theories but are actively discovering how science shapes the world around them. We are proud to provide our learners with the tools and environment to ignite their curiosity and fuel their passion for knowledge! Here's to more learning, growth, and scientific discoveries!
